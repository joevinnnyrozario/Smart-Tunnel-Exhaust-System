How to Interpret These Results

The confusion matrix is a key tool for evaluating classification model performance. For the AQI (Air Quality Index) detection system, here's what you want to see:

What's Good?
  • High TP: Model is good at detecting polluted air conditions
  • High TN: Model correctly identifies clean air conditions
  • Low FP: Few false alarms - model doesn't trigger unnecessarily
  • Low FN: Few missed detections - model rarely misses pollution
What's Bad?
  • High FP: Many false alarms - wastes time/resources
  • High FN: Many missed detections - critical safety issue
  • Low TP: Poor detection of actual pollution
  • Low TN: Poor recognition of safe conditions
